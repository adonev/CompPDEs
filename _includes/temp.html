Communication

We will try CampusWire for communication, and we will use github for sharing files and group work on assignments. The NYU Classes webpage will be used for important messages/announcements, as well as material that is for your personal use only such as sample solutions of past assignments. If you register for the class, you automatically have access. All course materials including lecture notes and assignments will be posted on this site as they become available.

You should feel free to email the instructor with any questions, concerns, or special requests such as deadline extensions, meeting outside of office hours, etc.
